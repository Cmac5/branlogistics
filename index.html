About us

Here at Bran Logistics we are “Focused on Safety. Delivering Service” Bran Logistics is family run and operating company, Based in Milperra Sydney. Customer Service and Safety are what we are all about. We can provide a large range of services and thrive to offer the best customer service. We can offer a run of services from Side loader deliveries to Specialist Tipping Skell deliveries and everything in between. We can also offer storage for containers amongst other things. With a hardstand area of approx. 8000sqm we can accommodate different types of products. Contact us to find out more. Our drivers have inductions to all the major NSW ports and each hold maritime security accreditation. We look forward to hearing how we can assist you with your next job, contact us today!
